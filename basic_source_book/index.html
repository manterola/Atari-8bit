DCF is a processor board to decode and display a signal from a DCF reciever,
and includes a programmable clock with four independant outputs.
Specs: 6502, 6532, 2716, 6 hex display, 7 LEDs, 7 push-buttons and 4 outputs,
all on two single-sided PCBs.
